About

How we got here

 

  • June 2016

    An Idea!

    Rohith Perumalla and Rohit Bolla, both Juniors in Frisco, Texas, get an idea to combine Rohith's digital experience and Rohit’s photography skill to create a business geared around capturing life’s best moments whilst integrating the incredible world of social media and world class editing software.

  • July 2016

    The Ball is Rolled

    Meeting at a local coffee shop in Frisco, the two friends work to create an effortless and clean website experience, discuss operating a clear cut business platform, and constructing a marketing approach utilizing the accessibility of social media apps such as Instagram, Twitter, and Linkedin.

  • August 2016

    Open for business!

    After a thorough process of constructing the foundation, Roho Squared goes live on \\date whenever we go live\\ and opens its services of high quality media.
